Who are we?

We are a medium-sized team who work to create amazing games that are maintained and of a high standard. We specialize in providing support to users, listening to your feedback, and just talking to everyone over on our Discord. We all work in our spare time from a remote location to optimize our collaboration. One of our main aims is to keep people informed about our progress and give our most dedicated players access to content in advance to allow them the opportunity to try it out and provide us with feedback.

Frequently Asked Questions

FAQ

Here are some of the most common questions we get asked, put here to save everyone time asking and answering them

Can I test out one of your games?

+

Yes, you sure can! If you navigate to the contact section below, and then choose to join our Discord, you can create a ticket within our server to get in touch with staff about testing.

What games do you make?

+

Mobile: Delta Space, Knightblade

Desktop: Blop, Faernox, Archimedes

Can I join your team?

+

Certainly. We're always on the lookout for new artists, developers, support agents and more! If you think you've got what it takes, then send us an email or join our Discord and open a ticket, and we'll get back to you. If accepted, you'll be added to our revshare system and given a split of the profit. Please note that some roles are voluntary (you can always ask us about this).

Do you do anything other than make games?

+

Yep, we also make Discord bots and some other, super secret (for now) stuff.

Are there any perks on joining your team?

+

Loads, it varies from role to role, but we offer the following perks to all staff:

- A custom business email (yourname@salukicorporation.com), usable in Gmail.

- Free Steam keys for you and your friends

- Free website hosting on our servers

Where can I get updates?

+

Updates are sent out on our Discord server. You can join that here

I have another question or need to contact you. How should I do it?

+

Use either of the contact options here. You'll normally get the fastest response via Discord, although we do usually check the email daily.
